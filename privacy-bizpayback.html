Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

CashFlow Logic Studios ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application BizPayback (the "App") handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

BizPayback operates as a strictly offline, local-storage application. All data you input (such as investment amounts, revenue projections, and cost items) is stored locally on your device using Hive database technology. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions only for specific functionalities:

  • Storage/Files: To generate and save PDF reports to your device (only when you explicitly click "Export").

3. Third-Party Services

Since the App is offline, we do not use third-party analytics services (like Firebase or Google Analytics) to track user behavior.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub page.
